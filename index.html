This Support / Resistance indicator for MT4 brings, besides displaying the SR price level for any timeframe chosen by the trade, also the possible future SR lines.

3 Lines Support / Resistance main functions

Further more, all this is done dynamically and automatically, so when the price reaches and closes a certain support or resistance line, this line is erased as not being valid anymore, and the possible SR line becomes the current level.

Chart examples



Blue solid lines are the support, red solid the resistance and the dotted lines are the possible support and resistance lines. The parameter of the LB indicator is controlled by the number of levels taken into account for penetration. When a certain number of points passes, the level lines on the indicated TF are pulled up.

3 Lines Support / Resistance Settings

Within the indicator settings, the trader has the option to enable which timeframes the SR lines and the future SR lines are to be displayed.
